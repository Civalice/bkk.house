DESCRIPTION

Limestone mansion duplex has been spectacularly restored. The stunning living room has original wood paneling and carvings and, French windows facing south with wonderful direct sunlight, a gorgeous decorator fireplace and soaring ceilings. The formal dining has huge leaded glass window and is also detailed with the original mantle. New chef's kitchen has separate dining area, stone counters and window. Gracious stairs take you to the bedrooms with grand master suite that includes a windowed dressing room or office, plus huge second bedroom that can be divided for a third bedroom. This home includes full laundry area, 2.5 baths, central AC, 14' ceilings in an elevator building.
